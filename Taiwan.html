I visited Taipei for the first time in January of 2023. I recommend going during this time of year for warm, but not hot weather.

  1. Taipei 101
  2. Jiu Fen
  3. Rao He Night Market

It is very easy to navigate the public transportation, there is wonderful, cheap streat food, and plenty of places to shop.
